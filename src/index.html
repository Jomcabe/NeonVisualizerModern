NEWON

Your music, painted in light.

Newon will ask macOS for permission to capture your Mac's audio, then react to whatever you're playing. Play something in Spotify and hit start.

NEWON

Let Newon hear your Mac

macOS routes system-audio capture through Screen Recording permission. In that list:

  1. Turn Newon on (first time only).
  2. Already shows enabled but still silent? The grant went stale after an update. Select Newon and click the “–” button to remove it — toggling off/on will not fix it.
  3. Fully quit and relaunch Newon, then add it back when asked.

Newon only reads the audio — it never records or transmits your screen.

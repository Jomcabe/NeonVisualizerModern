NEWON

A modern, neon music visualizer for your Mac.
It listens to your music and paints it in flowing light.

⬇ Download for macOS

Universal .dmg · Apple Silicon & Intel · macOS 13+

🎧 Hears your system audio (Spotify) 🌈 Neon ribbons + bloom 🌀 Kaleidoscope tunnel 🎵 Subtle now-playing + synced lyrics 🎚 Presets & sliders
First launch
1. Open the .dmg and drag Newon to Applications.
2. Right-click Newon → Open (it's unsigned, so bypass Gatekeeper once).
3. Hit Start Listening and grant Screen Recording permission — that's how macOS lets Newon capture system audio.
4. Play something in Spotify and enjoy. Press C for settings, F for fullscreen.
Open source · View on GitHub · Inspired by the Xbox 360 "Neon" visualizer.
